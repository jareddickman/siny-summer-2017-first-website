Agitation Flow

  1. Developer
    • Dependent on film and developing fluid
    • Agitate every 30 seconds
  2. Stop Bath
    • Agitate for a full 60 seconds
  3. Fixer
    • For 8 minutes, agitate every 30 seconds
  4. Wash
    • Rinse under running water for at least 2 minutes
